Timeline of Leadership Theories

1

Great Man Theory

Leaders are born with innate qualities and traits.

2

Trait Theory

Focus on identifying specific traits that make effective leaders.

3

Behavioral Theories

Emphasize the behaviors and actions of leaders rather than traits.

4

Contingency Theories

Leadership effectiveness depends on the situation and context.

5

Transformational Leadership

Leaders inspire and motivate followers to achieve extraordinary outcomes.

6

Servant Leadership

Leaders prioritize serving and supporting the needs of their team members.
